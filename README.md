# Resume Project Challenge 04: Provide **Insights** to **Management** in **Consumer Goods Domain**

## About

- Domain/Industry: Consumer Goods
- Function/Department: Executive Management

## Problem Statement

AtliQ Hardwares (imaginary company) is one of the leading computer hardware producers in India and has expanded well in other countries too.
However the management team lack actionable insights to make rapid data driven decisions.

To bridge this gap, as a participant in this challenge, my goal is to solve complex business queries (10 ad-hoc-requests), unmask insights, and
present the findings to executive stakeholders.
